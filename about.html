Our Story

Built by Taylor Morrison between 2014 and 2019, Arbor Lakes on Palmer Ranch is a friendly, family-oriented neighborhood in Sarasota, Florida. Known for its resort-style amenities, it offers a community pool, basketball courts, and a pirate-themed splash park. The area is peaceful, well-maintained, and close to top-rated schools. With its beautiful landscaping and welcoming atmosphere, Arbor Lakes is a great place to call home.
