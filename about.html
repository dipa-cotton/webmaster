Our Story

Built by Taylor Morrison between 2014 and 2019, Arbor Lakes on Palmer Ranch is a vibrant, family-oriented community in Sarasota, Florida. 217 acres off Honore Avenue, the neighborhood features 267 thoughtfully designed single-family homes with open floor plans and modern amenities, creating spaces where families can grow and thrive. Residents enjoy resort-style living with a community pool, basketball courts, and a pirate-themed splash park, encouraging both fun and connection. Surrounded by beautiful landscaping, top-rated schools, and a welcoming atmosphere, Arbor Lakes is a place where neighbors become friends, and every day offers a chance to feel at home.
