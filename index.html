ABOUT US

Located in Sarasota County on Palmer Ranch, Arbor Lakes is more than just a neighborhood - We are a community of people who care. Here, neighbors are friends, and together we build a place where everyone can thrive. We are dedicated to supporting one another, building lasting connections, and making arbor lakes a community full of energy, warmth, and possibility. Our commitment goes beyond houses and streets - it's about fostering a sense of belonging, pride, and shared purpose.
