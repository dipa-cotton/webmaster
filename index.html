ABOUT US

A neighborhood located in Sarasota county on Palmer Ranch, Arbor Lakes is a community dedicated to serving its members, built by Taylor Morrison between 2016 and 2019. Located on 217 acres off Honore Avenue, the neighborhood consists of 267 single family homes, designed with open floor plans and modern amenities. The community features a clubhouse, pool, basketball courts, and a pirate-themed splash park, providing residents with ample opportunities for recreation and socializing. Arbor Lakes is known for its beautiful landscaping, well-maintained common areas, and friendly atmosphere, making it a great place to call home.
